About Me

Hi! I'm Jones Mabala, a 32-year-old web developer based in Cagayan de Oro City, Misamis Oriental, Philippines. I'm happily married and a proud father to two beautiful daughters who are the light of my life. My family is my greatest source of happiness and motivation in everything I do.

Professionally, I specialize in building modern, responsive websites using WordPress and Elementor. With a passion for clean design and user-friendly functionality, I enjoy helping businesses and individuals bring their online presence to life.

Outside of work, I’m an active sports enthusiast. I love playing basketball, and I also enjoy volleyball and flag football. These outdoor activities keep me energized, disciplined, and balanced in both my personal and professional life.

I believe in lifelong learning, staying grounded, and doing everything with purpose—both in front of thescreen and out on the court.
